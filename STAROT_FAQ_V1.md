## What is Starot?
Starot is an upcoming tarot and astrology app built for Gen Z users who want something more personal, more healing-focused, and more emotionally useful.

## Is Starot a tarot app or an astrology app?
Both. Starot combines tarot and astrology in one experience.

## Who is Starot for?
Starot is designed for Gen Z and young adult users who want healing, companionship, emotional support, manifestation guidance, and better self-understanding.

## How is Starot different from Co-Star?
Starot is being built to feel warmer, more supportive, and more emotionally useful, while also combining tarot with astrology.

## How is Starot different from Moonly or Starla?
Starot is entering the market as a more Gen Z-centered, more personal, and more emotionally relevant option for users who want more than generic spiritual or horoscope content.

## Is Starot only for prediction?
No. Starot is also for reflection, emotional support, self-understanding, healing, and manifestation.

## Is Starot available now?
Not yet. Starot is currently in the waitlist stage.

## When is Starot launching?
Starot is planned to launch in April.

## Why join the waitlist?
Joining the waitlist gives users early access and first updates before public rollout.

## Is Starot for beginners?
Yes. Starot is being built to feel accessible, supportive, and easy to use for people new to astrology or tarot.
